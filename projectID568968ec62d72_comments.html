In the ongoing effort of documenting human delusions, this curious pamphlet is a nice example of how people can reason towards a certain conclusion, and basically refuse to accept any rational explanation or evidence that contradict their conclusion. Some flat-earth societies exist to this day, mostly fueled by religious fundamentalism, just as anti-evolution and climate change deniers often are, although they will often try to hide that fact.

This particular pamphlet was pretty rare, and hence the digitized version available was not entirely intact. I believe every word can be restored, so am running it through PP.
